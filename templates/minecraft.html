My Minecraft Mod: TitanMod v1.0


Description

TitanMod was created in Java 8 for Minecraft with the Forge Mod Loader.


Items

The Items in TitanMod are a TitanHammer, a TitanSword , a TitanDagger, a TitanKnife and a TitanPickaxe.


Tool Materials

The Tool Material Titanium is formed from a combination of Diamond and Redstone, depending on what form of Titanium you are trying to make.

Release

Version 1.0 of TitanMod is available fordownloadtoday!
